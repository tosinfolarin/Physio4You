EQUIPMENT

Resistance Band - £9.99

Resistance bands are elastic fitness tools versatile for strength training, flexibility, and rehabilitation. They provide resistance for muscle isolation or full-body workouts. They are portable and convenient, they are suitable for home workouts with various resistance levels, they cater to individuals of all fitness levels, promoting muscle activation .

Dumbbell - £10.50

Dumbbells are versatile free weights for strength training, offering a wide range of exercises for muscle development. Suitable for all fitness levels, they provide a convenient and effective way to build strength at home or in the gym.

Foam Roller - £15.00

Foam rollers are tools used for self-myofascial release (self-massage), aiding in muscle recovery and flexibility. By applying pressure to targeted areas, they help release muscle tightness and improve blood circulation. They can be used in warm-ups, cooldowns, and rehabilitation exercises, providing a cost-effective and portable solution for muscle care.

Yoga Mat - £7.99

Yoga mats provide a comfortable and non-slip surface for yoga and other floor exercises. They cushion joints, enhance stability, and create a dedicated space for mindful practices. Portable and easy to clean, yoga mats are essential for maintaining hygiene and focus during workouts.

Medicine Ball - £12.99

Medicine balls are weighted fitness tools used for strength training and dynamic exercises. They add resistance to a variety of movements, improving core strength, power, and coordination. Versatile in their applications, medicine balls are suitable for solo or partner workouts, providing an effective way to enhance both muscular and cardiovascular fitness.
